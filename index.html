01 / About

Who am I?

I'm an electronics engineer turned space nerd -- a cosmologist in the making! I'm a 3rd year PhD student at the Institute of Astronomy, University of Cambridge, funded by the Cambridge International & Isaac Newton Studentship. I work at the intersection of radio astronomy, machine learning, and Bayesian statistics to understand how the first stars and galaxies lit up the Universe.

My current research spans calibration techniques for the REACH radiometer under Dr. Eloy de Lera Acedo, and statistical modeling of X-ray binary luminosity distributions with Prof. Anastasia Fialkov. Before Cambridge, I completed my MS (Research) at IIT Indore, developing ML-assisted Bayesian pipelines for 21-cm cosmology with the SKA.

03 / Research

Research Work

REACH Calibration Bayesian Conjugate Priors Radio Astronomy

Calibration of the REACH Radiometer

PhD Research -- University of Cambridge | Supervisor: Dr. Eloy de Lera Acedo

Refining calibration techniques for the Radio Experiment for the Analysis of Cosmic Hydrogen (REACH) radiometer. The project focuses on finding the optimal band in the REACH system to perform absolute calibration using Bayesian Conjugate Priors.

X-ray Binaries Statistical Modeling 21-cm Simulations

X-ray Binary Luminosity Distributions & 21-cm Simulations

PhD Research -- University of Cambridge | Supervisors: Prof. Anastasia Fialkov & Dr. Boyuan Liu

Investigating the distribution of X-ray luminosities in X-ray binaries (XRBs) through innovative statistical modeling to understand the stochastic nature of XRB populations. Applying these insights to enhance 21-cm line simulations pivotal to the study of the Cosmic Dawn and Epoch of Reionization.

21-cm Cosmology SKA Machine Learning Bayesian Inference

ML-assisted Bayesian Pipeline for CD-EoR 21-cm Images

MS Thesis -- IIT Indore | Supervisors: Dr. Suman Majumdar & Prof. Abhirup Datta

Developed a Machine Learning-assisted Bayesian interpretation pipeline for Cosmic Dawn and Epoch of Reionization 21-cm tomographic images (to be produced by the SKA) using Largest Cluster Statistics (LCS) to understand the topological and morphological evolution of ionized hydrogen regions and draw inferences on ionizing sources and Inter-Galactic Medium properties.

SKA Data Challenge Radio Astronomy

SKA Science Data Challenge 3

SKA Science Working Group

Preparing the radio-astronomical community for next-generation data from the Square Kilometre Array. Working with realistic synthetic datasets simulating telescope capabilities to develop new analytical techniques for the order-of-magnitude increase in sensitivity.

Deep Learning CNN 21-cm

Neutral Fraction Prediction from HI 21-cm Images

Astro Hack Week 2022 -- Max Planck Institute for Astronomy

Developed a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) to predict the neutral fraction (xHI) directly from HI 21-cm images, providing a fast and accurate alternative to traditional analysis methods.

04 / Projects

Projects

REACH Radiometer Calibration Pipeline

Bayesian Conjugate Prior framework for absolute calibration of the REACH system, identifying optimal frequency bands for precision cosmological measurements.

XRB Luminosity Distribution Modeling

Statistical modeling of stochastic X-ray binary populations and their impact on 21-cm line simulations during the Cosmic Dawn and Epoch of Reionization.

ML-assisted Bayesian Pipeline for EoR 21-cm

End-to-end pipeline combining neural network emulators with MCMC sampling to interpret 21-cm tomographic images using Largest Cluster Statistics.

CNN for Neutral Fraction Prediction

Deep learning model trained on simulated 21-cm brightness temperature maps to predict the neutral hydrogen fraction during reionization.

Digital Michelson Interferometer

Arduino DUE-based digital interferometer using LDRs to measure thin silver coatings on glass slabs. Measured thickness of 0.174 μm vs. reported 0.25 μm.

Automated Star Tracking System

Cost-effective star tracker linking Stellarium to an analog telescope via Meade LX 200 protocol, Arduino DUE stepper motors, and an accelerometer.
